Sample Furnishing Checklist

Rough ranges in MAD. Ballparks to reach “Airbnb standard”.

Studio — ~20,000–28,000
  • Bed + mattress + linens: 6,000–9,000
  • Sofa/sofa-bed + coffee table: 3,500–5,000
  • Dining set (2–4): 1,500–3,000
  • Wardrobe/storage: 1,500–3,000
  • Lighting/curtains/rug: 1,500–2,500
  • Kitchen starter: 1,500–2,500
  • Decor: 1,000–2,000
  • Misc (hangers, hairdryer, iron, bathroom set): 1,000–2,000
1BR — ~30,000–45,000
  • Bedroom set: 8,000–12,000
  • Living room set: 6,000–9,000
  • Dining (table + 4 chairs): 2,500–4,500
  • Extra linens/towels: 2,000–3,500
  • Kitchen expansion: 2,000–4,000
  • Decor & lighting: 2,000–4,000
  • Misc & spares: 2,000–4,000
2BR — ~45,000–70,000
  • Two bedroom sets: 16,000–24,000
  • Living room: 8,000–12,000
  • Dining (table + 6 chairs): 4,000–6,000
  • Kitchen full kit: 4,000–6,000
  • Decor & lighting: 3,000–6,000
  • Misc & spares: 3,000–6,000

Tip: keep receipts & SKU links. Photograph the apartment after setup for your insurance file.
